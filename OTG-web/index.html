ABOUT US

On The Go Distribution, Inc. officially went into business in May of 2010. On The Go was founded to distribute the H10O Vitamin Infused Flavored Water beverage line of products. Our warehouse is located in Perry Hall, Maryland.

On The Go was started by Robin and Marty Eason. Robin and Marty have owned several successful businesses and currently own SunSeekers, Inc. and SunBed Service and Supply, Inc. These businesses serve individual tanning clients in Maryland as well as tanning salon businesses in the USA and overseas.

The mission of On The Go is quite simple; H10O Vitamin Infused Flavored Water beverage products are the best-tasting and best for you enhanced water products available today. Our goal is to make people aware of them by getting them into as many distribution channels as possible.

If you are a beverage retailer or distributor interested in H10O please visit our SALES page for more information.
