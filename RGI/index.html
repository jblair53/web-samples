Ray Group International LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small business dedicated to providing world-class service to our customers, partners, and employees. We specialize in offering a full range of healthcare-related IT, management, and communications support services to clients within the Federal Government, the private sector and abroad. We believe in doing things the right way – and we take pride in managing our business, partnerships, and professional relationships with this philosophy. We focus foremost on service – internally and externally – assured that good things will happen as a result.
