Community Involvement

Ray Group International is honored to lend its support to American veterans and their families through its charitable contributions to the following organizations:

Tragedy Assistance Program for Survivors (TAPS)

TAPS is a premier provider of comprehensive care to the families and friends of military servicemembers lost in the line of duty. To date, the association has provided grief and trauma counseling, crisis intervention and case work assistance to over 30,000 individuals. Ray Group is a proud sponsor of the TAPS Good Grief Camp for children and teens struggling with the loss of loved one.

taps.org

Supporting America's Finest, Inc.

Supporting America's Finest, Inc., is a non-profit dedicated to honoring and commemorating the sacrifices of servicemembers while raising funds for veterans healthcare. The organization orchestrates musical concerts to benefit active and retired military personnel.

supportingamericasfinest.com

Congressional Medal of Honor Foundation

The Medal of Honor Foundation is dedicated to preserving the legacy of America's highest military honor through commemorative initiatives and awareness campaigns. In March 2011, Ray Group International was fortunate to sponsor and host a reception in honor of the 150th Anniversary of the Medal of Honor for all surviving recipients and their families.
