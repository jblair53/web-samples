IT Development & Support

Ray Group provides extensive, state-of-the-art Information Technology (IT) products and services in software development, enterprise architecture, system conversion, and project support. Our solutions are tailored to meet each individual customer’s IT system requirements in a wide variety of managerial, administrative, engineering, and developmental applications.

RGI ensures the success of IT product development by providing the professional support services to manage the complex requirements s that are necessary to capture, launch, and administer overarching programs. We offer disaster recovery and continuity of operations planning, requirements analysis and management, decision support modeling and analysis (including cost), and program management.

In addition, RGI offers clients systems and engineering support throughout all phases of the software development lifecycle (SDLC). From initial technology assessment and analysis, through development, integration, implementation, and maintenance, RGI’s systems and software engineers have the experience, expertise, tools, and methodologies required to successfully execute complex projects.

Our team of highly skilled advisors offers expert IT strategic consulting services on capital planning, implementation planning, risk analysis and management by helping our customers manage IT investments throughout the investment lifecycle. They also ensure a maximum return on each investment, eliminate redundant capabilities, and bridge the gap between planning and execution.

To ensure deliverables align with the customer’s needs and business goals, our dedicated teams approach the SDLC through the application of an agile methodology that allows for frequent inspection, adaptation, teamwork, accountability, rapid delivery, and constant feedback.

RGI’s core competencies in IT Services include:

  • Systems and Software Development and Support
  • Enterprise Architecture
  • Strategic Planning and Support
  • Analysis of Existing Technology Solutions
  • Web Application Design and Development
  • Portal Development and Support
  • IT Products and Services Training
