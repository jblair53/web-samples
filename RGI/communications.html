Communications

Communications is a key ingredient in fostering necessary and vital collaboration within an organization. Ray Group provides strategic communications development, analysis, planning, design, and support. RGI's technical and subject matter experts have in-depth Federal government experience and a thorough understanding of emerging business and technology applications.

The RGI communications team has extensive experience in developing and delivering presentations, technical writing and editing, graphic design, website development, knowledge management, social media development, internal and external communications, meeting facilitation, and support for overall communications strategies. Our communications team schedules and facilitates meetings, prepares briefings, and conducts data gathering interviews.

Initially we work to foster a creative and collaborative relationship with our customers in order to achieve their goals and objectives. Our approach is based on a thorough and detailed understanding of how the major components of an organization must work in concert with each other to help ensure success on all fronts and with all audiences. RGI's core competencies in Strategic Communications Services and Support include:

  • Strategic Communications Planning and Development
  • Technical Writing and Editing
  • Multimedia Development
  • Meeting Facilitation and Support
  • Organizational Outreach
