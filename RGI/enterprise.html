Enterprise Architecture

Ray Group employs a multi-disciplined team of Enterprise Architects (EA) who analyze the structure, enterprise components, and relationships in an organization with the goal of improving the efficiency of the enterprise as a whole. Over the last few years, the productivity of government agencies has become increasingly dependent on the efficiency and interoperability of their IT systems. Ray Group’s approach to Enterprise Architecture and the application of open system engineering standards provide a framework for streamlining an organization’s IT assets under one comprehensive umbrella.

RGI’s systems engineering and software architecture capabilities include architecture analysis, architectural direction, and architecture and design reviews. We work closely with customers and partners, using an interdisciplinary process to ensure that customer and stakeholder needs are met in a timely and cost-efficient manner.

RGI’s core competencies in systems engineering and enterprise architecture include:

  • Architecture Analysis, Planning, and Support
  • Architecture Development
  • Architecture Transition Planning
  • Architecture Reviews
