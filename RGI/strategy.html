Strategic Planning & Development

Ray Group is a leader in strategic planning and operations support. Identifying stakeholders early in a project, clarifying project objectives, allocating skilled resources quickly, collaborating with the customer and project teams, and incremental analysis keeps us ahead of project risks and ensures a successful project deployment.

Ray Group is committed to the integrity of live project environments, information assurance, and bridges the gap between IT and business operations. Comprehensive integration activities ensure that project teams are informed and projects deploy on-time. 

Our operations planning approach is based upon a clearly defined strategy that lays the groundwork for oversight and clear understanding of the approaches, methods, and techniques for delivery and execution of a successful project deployment. 

Operations capabilities includes application design, planning, scheduling, communications, release and deployment management, monitoring, training, service desk, and governance for large-scale deployments. Ray Group uses standard tools, processes, and metrics to manage configuration control and budget guidelines which ensures project success on multiple levels.

RGI’s core competencies in Strategic Planning and Operations Support include:

  • Integrated Project Teams
  • Ongoing Analysis and Performance Metrics
  • Deployment, Release, and Configuration Management
  • Application Monitoring
  • Application Training
  • Disaster Recovery
  • Technical Service Desk Support
